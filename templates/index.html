Plan your European Holiday!

While we're stuck in COVID-induced lockdown in Melbourne, can anyone really blame you for wanting to plan your next holiday?

And where better to holiday than Europe – the delectable buffet of options can hit your craving, whether that is plundering pizza and pasta in Milan, crunching on croissants and coffee^ in Paris or sipping steins^^ and sweet strudels in Vienna.

In order to whet your appetite, have fun perusing some images of the most popular cities below, before diving into some Country Comparisons.

Is safety important to you or will you be diving headfirst out of a plane in Lauterbrunnen, Switzerland? Is pollution important to avoid or are you the type to hire a Humvee in Munich and drive through the Black Forest?

Finally we've done some of the hard work for you and investigated a handful of the most popular cities. Zoom in and take a look around at the restaurants, hotels and train stations. Of course this is a luxury holiday so no budget backpackers or dodgy diners are included on our map – rest assured it's 5 stars all the way.

So come away with us!

^ As a true Melburnian please be warned about European coffee… Melbourne truly is the home of great coffee. But rest assured if you're from Sydney you'll probably enjoy it just fine.

^^ Be doubly warned - if you do not drink European beer in Australia, it may ruin Carlton Draught or Victoria Bitter for you... the European Beer selection at your local Dan Murphy's is there for a reason.
