Country Statistics

The above chart details an expected safety rating for countries throughout Europe. These are based on pollution level; crime rate and the nations trust in their law enforcement.

The foundation for each country is information on the class breakdown (that is why the bottom three colours all add up to 100). These of course are represented by upper, middle, and lower classes. There is no value judgement made on these numbers, they are simply there to inform the information that comes above and may impact your final decision.

Air breathability, safety from crime and trust in the police are all scores out of 100.

Essentially the higher the bar the safer the country is, as all data points are interpreted in a way that make the higher score best.

Keep in mind that safety is never guaranteed on a holiday, and you must still be a discerning traveller but hopefully with the above in mind you can make the choice that will best line up with your expectation.
